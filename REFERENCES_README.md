## üìñ Citation Examples

### APA 7th Style:
```
Mosia, M., Egara, F. O., Nannim, F. A., & Basitere, M. (2025). 
Bayesian Hierarchical Modelling of Student Academic Performance: 
The Impact of Mathematics Competency, Institutional Context, and 
Temporal Variability. Education Sciences, 15(2), 177. 
https://doi.org/10.3390/educsci15020177
```

### IEEE Style:
```
[1] M. Mosia, F. O. Egara, F. A. Nannim, and M. Basitere, 
"Bayesian Hierarchical Modelling of Student Academic Performance: 
The Impact of Mathematics Competency, Institutional Context, and 
Temporal Variability," Education Sciences, vol. 15, no. 2, 
p. 177, 2025, doi: 10.3390/educsci15020177.
```

### In-text citation:
```
Recent work has applied hierarchical Bayesian models to student 
performance data (Mosia et al., 2025), demonstrating the 
effectiveness of multilevel approaches for educational outcomes.
```
